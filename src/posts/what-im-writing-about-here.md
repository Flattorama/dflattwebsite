I run marketing across five brands at Canada's longest-running startup accelerator, and I build the AI systems that make that possible — the agents, the workflows, and the team structures around them. This blog is where I write the long-form version of that work.

Four themes to expect:

**The AI-native marketing org.** Org design, workforce planning with AI, agent architectures for marketing teams, and the build-vs-buy decisions that actually matter. This is the flagship thread.

**Playbooks and teardowns.** Real workflows with the architecture shown — how a backlog got cleared, how a brand-voice pipeline is wired, what broke and what shipped.

**Governed AI.** I trained as a litigator before I ran marketing teams. Brand safety, compliance-aware automation, and what marketers actually get sued for — written from both sides of that fence.

**Multi-brand and ecosystem growth.** Portfolio marketing architecture, accelerator go-to-market, and the franchise and CAC case studies behind the numbers I quote on stage.

New essays land here first, then get syndicated. If you'd rather get them in your inbox, the [LinkedIn newsletter](https://www.linkedin.com/in/danielflatt/) is the place. And if any of this is relevant to your stage or your team — [I speak about it too](/speaking).
